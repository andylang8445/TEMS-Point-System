TEMS Integrated Reward System

Academic Points

  • Perfect attendance (9:30am and 3:30pm) = 5 points a day
  • Participate in any AMP class = 5 points each AMP class
  • Teacher’s recommendation = 15 points each recommendation
  • Teacher’s negative report = -5 points each report
  • 10 minutes or more late a class/AMP = -5 points
  • Missing a class/AMP = -10 points each class


Social Contribution Points

  • Participate in a group activity = 5 points
  • Participate in a weekly challenge = 10 points
  • Participate in volunteering activities = 20 points
  • Win a weekly challenge = 50 points
  • Win a game night = DOUBLE the existing social points!


Prizes

  • Every two weeks we will host an Online Crazy Night at which you can bet your points to try and earn the most
  • TOP 5 Students with the most points at the end of the Crazy Night will receive one time extra money ($30) to their weekly allowance and a gift ($20) from TEMS
  • The Next 10 students with the most points will receive one time extra money ($30) to their weekly allowance
